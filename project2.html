MAGAZINE

I worked with two others to design a magazine layout about the topic of Samoyeds in hopes of spreading awareness and showcase the beauty and importance of animals. This project showcases demonstrates my graphic design and photoshop skills.

Launch Project

Type: Design

Category: Entertainment

Deliverables: Graphic

RESEARCH

Prior to laying out the content of the magazine, we researched about the stakeholders and goals we wanted to achieve from this outcome. We did extensive research about different types of magazine such as entertainment and geographic. Moving forward, we tested different font types, colors, and font size that would best suit the theme.

PROGRESS

After collecting research content and decided on the overall style of the magazine, we brainstormed on various layouts that would best suit our audience. I used illustrator to create frameworks of all the possible frameworks for the magazine. Furthermore, I consider hierarchy and placement of elements to make sure user experience is captured throughout reading through the magazine

GRAPHIC DESIGN

Our team decided on the best layout that would best tell the story and proceeded to combine all the images and text into the magazine.

CONCLUSION

This project successfully demonstrates my graphic design skills. The most difficult part was researching and making sure that each decision was made in the best interest for our targeted audience. What made this project so succcessful was our team work and dedication.
